Spanish-American dual citizen with an engineering and robotics background and over nine years of experience across the US, UK, and European automation industries.

I build commercial operations from the ground up — from co-founding Bastian Solutions Europe and growing its revenue from zero to €6M, to leading UK & Ireland sales at Toyota Automated Logistics, where I secured multi-million-euro contracts with some of Britain's most recognised brands.

Today I serve as Sales Director, Western Europe at Sereact, where I lead commercial strategy for Spain, France, and the UK. Sereact builds AI software that enables robots to autonomously pick, sort, inspect, and handle returns — transforming how the world's warehouses operate.

I am passionate about the future of robotics and embodied AI as drivers of new economic growth — and about bridging the gap between complex technical capability and real commercial value.
